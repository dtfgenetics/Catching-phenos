# Game Bible

# PhenoQuest: The Living Seed Vault

## One-Sentence Pitch

PhenoQuest is an original cannabis genetics creature-collection RPG where players battle wild Phenos for Cuttings, root clones, identify Keepers, reveal genotypes, make crosses, grow environmental expressions, and restore the Living Seed Vault before Team Lockout replaces living variation with sterile synthetic clones.

## Core Identity

- **Public title:** PhenoQuest: The Living Seed Vault
- **Tagline:** Collect the Whole Garden
- **Internal motto:** Cloning preserves. Crossing discovers.
- **World:** Verdantia
- **Player role:** Vault Runner / Pheno Hunter
- **Creatures:** Phenos
- **Collection system:** PhenoLog
- **Clone station:** Clone Dome
- **Home/collection hub:** Vault Garden
- **Breeding station:** Lineage Lab
- **Main villain faction:** Team Lockout

## Design Pillars

1. **Genotype is the blueprint. Environment decides the expression.**
2. **Wild Phenos are not caught directly; players battle for Cuttings and root clones.**
3. **The goal is not only collecting species — it is finding Keepers.**
4. **Breeding/crossing reveals new combinations and restores lost lineages.**
5. **Every rooted clone, expression, Keeper, and lineage repairs the Living Seed Vault.**
6. **Team Lockout removes variation through cloning abuse, synthetic control, and genetic ownership.**
7. **The game must remain original, parody-safe, data-driven, and expandable.**

## Player Goal Ladder

1. **Battle for Cuttings**
2. **Root your Clones**
3. **Find your Keepers**
4. **Read the Genotype**
5. **Make the Cross**
6. **Grow the Expression**
7. **Restore the Lineage**

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Explore a region.
2. Encounter a wild Pheno.
3. Use Grow Battle mechanics.
4. Earn Cuttings and possible expression-tagged samples.
5. Collect enough Cuttings for a clone recipe.
6. Start clone propagation in the Clone Dome.
7. Wait for the real-time rooting timer.
8. Reveal clone quality, trait, expression, and stability.
9. Add the Rooted Clone to the team or Vault Garden.
10. Mark strong clones as Keepers.
11. Use genotypes and Keepers for future Lineage Lab crosses.
12. Restore Vault branches and region health.

## Story Premise

The world of Verdantia is protected by the Living Seed Vault, a natural archive that stores the genetic echoes of every Pheno lineage. Team Lockout is damaging the Vault, clamping wild expressions, stealing Vault Tags, and replacing living diversity with sterile synthetic clones.

Professor Calyx recruits the player as a new Vault Runner. The player must travel through Verdantia, collect Cuttings responsibly, root stable clones, identify Keepers, restore lost lineages, and stop Team Lockout from overwriting the Vault with the Null Cultivar.

## Core Science-Fantasy Rule

Every Pheno has:

1. **Species** — what it is.
2. **Genotype** — hidden genetic potential.
3. **Expression** — visible traits shaped by environment.
4. **Lineage** — its place in the Living Seed Vault.
