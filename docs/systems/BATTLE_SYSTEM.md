# Grow Battle System

## System Purpose

Grow Battles are how players interact with wild Phenos, Team Lockout enemies, Trial Keepers, and corrupted lineages.

Battles are not only about reducing health. They are about managing **Vigor**, **Stability**, expression effects, and Cutting rewards.

## Core Stats

| Stat | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Vigor | health / ability to continue |
| Power | direct attack strength |
| Terps | special move strength and expression effects |
| Roots | defense and recovery |
| Speed | turn order and evasion |
| Stability | control, stress resistance, clone quality influence |

## Signature Resource: Stability

Stability represents genetic balance, trust, and control.

Low Stability can cause:

- move failure
- lower Cutting quality
- unstable clone results
- harder clone rooting
- higher corruption risk
- stronger Team Lockout effects

Team Lockout specializes in attacking Stability.

## MVP Battle Actions

| Action | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Strike | reliable direct damage |
| Express | class-based special move |
| Root Down | defend and regain Stability |
| Observe | reveal class, temperament, Stability, expression clue, or trait clue |
| Item | use care, battle, or clone-related items |
| Swap | change active Pheno |

No direct catch action should exist.

## Wild Battle Result

Wild battles award Cuttings rather than captured creatures.

Reward factors:

- win condition
- remaining player Stability
- use of Observe
- weather and environment cue
- over-damage penalty
- corruption cleanse
- trial or quest modifiers

## Battle Types

### Wild Pheno Battle

Goal: earn Cuttings, Trait Samples, and Expression Tags.

### Team Lockout Battle

Goal: defeat synthetic/corrupted Phenos and disable Lockout machines.

### Garden Trial Battle

Goal: test understanding of local mechanics, often with a puzzle condition.

### Corrupted Pheno Battle

Goal: cleanse or weaken corruption to earn usable material.

## MVP Status Effects

| Status | Effect |
|---|---|
| Wilted | loses Vigor each turn |
| Rootbound | Speed reduced |
| Overfed | Terp moves weakened |
| Locked Out | cannot use items temporarily |
| Unstable | Power rises but Stability drops |

|---|---|---|---|
| Fruit | Skunk | Frost | speed, aroma, burst |
| Skunk | Haze | Fruit | disruption and stress |
| Haze | Kush | Skunk | speed and turn control |

## Design Rule

Battles should support clone quality and progression. A battle is not isolated combat; it produces the material used by the cloning and breeding systems.
